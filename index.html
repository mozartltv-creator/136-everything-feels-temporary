Why Does Everything Suddenly Feel Temporary?

Core Thesis

Sudden awareness of impermanence can destabilize emotional security. When continuity feels uncertain, attachment recalibrates.

Main Answer

When impermanence becomes conscious, stability can feel fragile. The structure of reality has not changed — perception has.

1. Background Assumption of Stability

Daily life assumes continuity until awareness shifts.

2. Attachment Under Threat

Attachment relies on perceived durability.

3. Heightened Sensitivity

Time may feel more visible and emotionally charged.

4. The Shock of Non-Permanence

Realizing nothing is fixed can feel destabilizing.

5. Control vs Reality

We prefer stability, but reality is dynamic.

6. Existential Adjustment

Impermanence can increase anxiety or presence.

7. The Structural Boundary

Impermanence reframes meaning. It does not eliminate it.
